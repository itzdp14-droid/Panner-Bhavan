Things to Discover

Main Falls (Peraruvi)

0.5 km · 5 min walk

The crown jewel of Courtallam — a majestic 60-metre waterfall whose herb-rich waters are said to have powerful healing and medicinal properties. Open 24 hours in peak season.

Five Falls (Aintharuvi)

4 km · 10 min drive

Water dramatically splits into five distinct streams before cascading down. Surrounded by lush forest with fruit stalls selling mangosteen, lychee, and jackfruit.

Thirukuttralanathar Temple

0.3 km · 3 min walk

One of the oldest Shiva temples in South India, dating to the Pandya and Chola kingdoms. Ancient inscriptions on its walls tell a thousand years of devotion.

Old Courtallam Falls

8 km · 15 min drive

A hidden gem — a 200-ft waterfall en route to Tenkasi, framed by lush rice fields, coconut groves, and mango trees. Far less crowded and truly serene.
