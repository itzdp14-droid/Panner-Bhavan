🗺 Explore

Nearby Attractions

Discover the wonders of Courtallam and beyond

💧 Waterfalls
🌊
Courtallam Main Falls
The crown jewel of Courtallam — a majestic 60-metre waterfall with herb-rich medicinal waters. Just 0.5 km away!
💦
Five Falls (Aintharuvi)
Water splits into five dramatic streams. Surrounded by fruit stalls with mangosteen & jackfruit. 4 km away.
🏞️
Old Courtallam Falls
A hidden gem — 200-ft waterfall near Tenkasi, framed by rice fields and coconut groves. Serene and less crowded.
✨
Shenbagadevi Falls
A beautiful waterfall surrounded by lush green forest — peaceful and picturesque at any time of year.
🛕 Temples
🛕
Kasi Viswanathar Temple
Ancient Shiva temple in Tenkasi, just 6 km away. One of the most revered temples in Tirunelveli district.
⛩️
Kutralanathar Temple
One of the oldest Shiva temples in South India, dating to the Pandya and Chola kingdoms. Just 0.3 km away.
🙏
Aryankavu Sastha Temple
A famous temple on the scenic Aryankavu Pass road — an essential stop on the Kerala route.
🌿 Nature & Scenic
🌅
Palaruvi Falls
A stunning 91-metre waterfall near the Kerala border — a wonderful day trip through the Western Ghats.
🌻
Surandai Sunflower Fields
Seasonal attraction — golden sunflower fields stretching to the horizon. A photographer's paradise.
🏔️
Western Ghats Viewpoints
Breathtaking mountain vistas, trekking trails and misty hills — nature at its most spectacular.
